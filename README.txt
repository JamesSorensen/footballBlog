Project: mini-blog
Name: Football Blog

Description:
 In our blog you will find a lot of useful and up-to-date information. 
 Read publications from experts in world of football, or publish 
 your own interesting articles. Discuss publications, share your thoughts
 and found friends and interlocutors.


Platform: NodeJS + Express
Database: MongoBD
Template engine: EJS
Preprocessor: Sass ( scss )
Dropper: Gulp 4


Additional libraries:

Backend:
 Mongoose
 https://mongoosejs.com/

 Bcrypt
 https://www.npmjs.com/package/bcrypt

 Moment
 https://www.npmjs.com/package/moment

 Transliter
 https://www.npmjs.com/package/transliter

FrontEnd

 jQuery
 https://jquery.com/

 FontAwesome
 https://fontawesome.com/


Functional:

 Personal account
 User registration ( Login & Password )
 Create, delete and edit posts
 Discussion of publications, system of comments
